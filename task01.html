Top 5 places to visit in India

  1. Leh:
    Located at a height of 11,500 feet, Leh finds place in the bucket list of many travel enthusiasts. It will leave you in awe with its iconic monasteries and picturesque landscapes.
  2. Gokarna:
    Home to pristine beaches bordered by swaying palms and renowned Hindu shrines, Gokarna is an unspoilt temple town in Karnataka. It lies close to popular tourist destinations like Goa, Mangalore and Hubli.
  3. Srinagar
    Jahangir’s ‘heaven on earth’, Srinagar offers the picturesque Dal Lake, breath-taking mountain views, stunning Mughal gardens and sprawling orchards.
  4. Havelock Islands
    Also known as Swaraj Island, Havelock Island is a part of Ritchie’s Archipelago in the Andamans. Its dazzling white sand beaches and fascinating coral reefs are admired by nature lovers from around the world.
  5. Puducherry
    A fascinating, small town with enough elements to charm its visitors, Pondicherry whose official name was changed to Puducherry in 2006, was once the largest French colony in India.
